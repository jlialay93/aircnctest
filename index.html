About this dog

Lumo (pronounced Lou-MOE) is always ready to play, play, play! Once he has had his share of fetch and wrestling his toys, he loves to snuggle. Personal-Space is not a concept in Lumo's world. So, if you are looking for some cuddles, Lumo's your guy! Lumo is pure mutt. Son of a free-roaming farm dog from Virgina, we aren't really sure who is in his family tree. However, we do know that he has big, adorable ears perfect for petting and a squishy body that you'll love to hug.
